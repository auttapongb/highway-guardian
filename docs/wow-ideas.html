The 4 Ideas

🥇 #1 Most Buildable + Wow
🔊 Acoustic AI Road Monitoring
"ถนนที่ได้ยิน" — The Road That Hears

A distributed network of Raspberry Pi + USB microphone arrays along highways that detect, classify, and localize crashes, honking, tire screech, engine anomalies, and vehicle types by sound alone. No cameras. No privacy issues. Works at night, in rain, in fog.

🔥 Wow: 9/10 💰 ~15K THB ⏱️ 6 weeks 🏷️ C4 Safety · C6 Digital

Tech Stack

RPi 4 + USB mic array (~2,800 THB/node)
4 nodes along 500m highway
YAMNet/TinyML on-device
TDOA crash localization
MQTT + dashboard

Why Nobody in Thailand Has Done This

  • France (Cerema) — experimental acoustic road monitoring
  • Norway (SINTEF) — bridge/tunnel acoustic traffic
  • USA (ShotSpotter) — acoustic gunshot detection in cities
  • Thailand/APAC: ZERO deployments
  • DOH uses CCTV (vision-only). No sound analysis exists.

🎬 Demo: The Blindfold Test

Set up 4 microphone nodes in the demo hall. Play crash sound on hidden speaker → dashboard lights up: "🚨 CRASH DETECTED — Km 23+400 — Alert sent" + LINE notification popup. The audience hears the crash and sees the system react before anyone can click a button. Then play a different sound (honk vs tire screech vs motorcycle) — system classifies each correctly. The hook: "This works with your eyes closed."

🥈 #2 Most Practical + Low Cost
📳 Audio/Vibration Pothole Detection
"ทุกหลุมบ่อมีเสียง" — Every Pothole Has a Sound Signature

A smartphone app or $5 roadside IoT sensor that detects potholes by analyzing the unique vibration + audio signature when a vehicle hits them. No computer vision. Just the thud. TinyML on-device classifies: pothole vs speed bump vs manhole vs rumble strip.

🔥 Wow: 8.5/10 💰 ~5-15K THB ⏱️ 6 weeks 🏷️ C3 Maintenance · C4 Safety

Two Implementation Options

Option A: Phone App
DOH employees mount phones on dashboard → accelerometer + mic records at 100Hz → TinyML on-device → GPS-tagged potholes uploaded to map.

Option B: Roadside IoT
RPi Pico W (350 THB) + MEMS accelerometer (150 THB) embedded in roadside poles → detects vehicle impacts → solar-powered.

Why Nobody in Thailand Has Done This

  • Toyota, Hyundai, Nissan — research papers only (2018-2023)
  • India's "Pothole Patrol" — GPS + accelerometer on buses (acoustic not used)
  • Thailand: ZERO acoustic/vibration pothole systems
  • DOH does visual inspection only
  • No Thai startup or academic project has built this

🎬 Demo: The "Thud" That Saves Roads

A small ramp with a pothole mockup. Team member rides bicycle over it → "THUD." Big screen shows: waveform of impact → "POTHOLE DETECTED — Severity: MEDIUM — GPS: 13.736°N" popping up on Bangkok map → heatmap layer adds this pothole. Then: ride over speed bump → "SPEED BUMP — NOT a pothole." The hook: "We don't need cameras to see potholes. We only need to listen."

🥉 #3 Most Mind-Blowing (Medium Risk)
🛰️ Satellite AI + InSAR for Highway Deformation
"มองจากอวกาศ" — Watching from Space

Automated processing of free Sentinel-1 satellite radar data using AI to detect millimeter-level road surface deformation, subsidence, and landslide precursors across Thailand's highway network. No ground sensors. No cameras. Just space.

🔥 Wow: 10/10 💰 ~20-30K THB ⏱️ 6 weeks (pre-process data) 🏷️ C3 Maintenance · C5 Sustainable

Tech Stack

Sentinel-1 SAR (ESA, free)
ISCE2 + MintPy → deformation time series
Anomaly detection autoencoder
Folium dashboard + time-lapse
Google Earth Engine for data access

Why Nobody in Thailand Has Done This

  • Japan (JAXA + NEXCO) — operational highway InSAR monitoring
  • China (Gaofen constellation) — road monitoring from space
  • Europe (ESA) — operational services for highway agencies
  • Thailand: ZERO — GISTDA focuses on agriculture/flood
  • DOH has no satellite-based monitoring at all
  • No Thai startup, academic paper, or govt project combines InSAR + AI for highways

🎬 Demo: The X-Ray from Space

30-second time-lapse animation: Bangkok Outer Ring Road 2023→2026 in false-color radar deformation. Areas sinking >10mm/year glow red. The audience watches a road section progressively sink — invisible to the naked eye. Zoom in: "KM 12+400 to KM 12+800 — Deformation: 14mm/year — Risk: HIGH — First detected: March 2024 — Trend: ACCELERATING." The punchline: "This is happening RIGHT NOW. Every road has an X-ray from space. We just never looked."

4 #4 Cheapest + Most Scalable
🔌 Acoustic Beamforming Crash Localization
"เสียงเรียกหาเมื่อเกิดเหตุ" — The Sound That Calls for Help

Ultra-low-cost $5 ESP32 microphone nodes spaced every 50m along highway guardrails. Using time-difference-of-arrival beamforming to localize crashes within 2-3 meters. Auto-triggers LINE emergency alerts. Solar + battery = 2-year life. At 1/100th the cost of CCTV cameras.

🔥 Wow: 9/10 💰 ~8-12K THB (6 nodes) ⏱️ 6 weeks 🏷️ C4 Safety · C6 Digital

Per-Node Cost Breakdown

ESP32 board: 250 THB
MEMS microphone: 50 THB
18650 battery x2: 200 THB
2W solar panel: 150 THB
Total: ~650 THB/node

Gateway (RPi Zero + 4G): 2,000 THB
6 nodes + gateway: ~5,900 THB

Why Nobody in Thailand Has Done This

  • USA (ShotSpotter) — $1B company for urban acoustic gunshot detection
  • France (Cerema) — experimental acoustic guardrail research
  • Thailand/APAC: ZERO crash localization via sound
  • DOH emergency response: 15-30 min average via calls to 1669
  • 1 CCTV camera = ~50,000 THB; our node = 650 THB

🎬 Demo: Instant Crash Alert

6 mic nodes placed in a 20m line. Team member crashes metal objects together at an unmarked location. Within 1 second: each node LED lights up → dashboard shows crash pin matching actual location within 30cm → simulated LINE message: "🚗 CRASH ALERT: Highway 1, Km 23+200 — Emergency services notified." Then play noise-canceled audio of just the crash. The hook: "Each node costs less than a highway cone. One crash that goes undetected costs lives."
